A Poem to my Oldest Friend

The Red Giant

Dearest Apollo,

my friend
you have many diffrent auras
there exists a deep admiration
for your diffrent personalities
your rays
make us more spiritual
sleepier
happier
our grateful hands
rejoice in your light
you stroke our skin
feel between our eyes
inner sense
pure light and innocence
a freckle
you are as bright as we
want you to be
you are heavenly
Yours truly, Dominique
